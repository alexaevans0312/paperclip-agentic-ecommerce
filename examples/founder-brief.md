# Founder Brief Example

## Goal

Launch Squish's first pimple patch offer with a bundle-led Shopify funnel and creator seeding plan while keeping blended CAC under $18 and all customer-facing claims cosmetic-safe.

## Business context

Squish is a pre-launch skincare ecommerce brand launching premium visible hydrocolloid pimple patches. The first SKU is a 36-40 count pack at a target retail price of $16.

## Constraints

- Hydrocolloid-only first SKU.
- No acne-drug actives.
- No medical claims.
- Keep launch AOV above $28 through bundles.
- Preserve Squish's cute-but-credible positioning.
- Do not send outreach or spend money without founder approval.

## Desired output

- Offer structure.
- Funnel map.
- Creator acquisition plan.
- Compliance-safe messaging.
- Stripe action plan for any required tools/provisioning/payments.
- Approval ledger.
- Founder next-actions list.
